SignCube is an online database developed for various sign-languages around the world.

SignCube aims to provide:

  • an online platform for documenting endagered Sign Languages around the world

  • a supporting Sign Language teaching service through an extensive and categorized dictionary

  • a platform for comparing the properties of different Sign Languages

  • a sophisticated system that signifies the importance of collaboration between Deaf and Hearing people

  • Accessibility

    Everything is safely stored in the cloud, so that you can view it anywhere anytime. If you wish to assist in making SignCube more diverse you can also upload from anywhere around the world with our Mobile App.

  • Research

    With further funding this website will be improved to include, a dynamic teching service which remembers the words you have viewed, and allows connection of your own camera to view how you are performing the sign.

  • Links

    Click here to find links to associated websites and organisations who helped make this project possible.

  • Events

    Upcoming events, held be organisations around the world will be listed here, so that you will not miss out on anything around your community.

  • Teaching

    As a student you can easily search for any word or phrase in any of our Sign-Languages from all around the world, and even compare it to another Sign-Language from another country.
